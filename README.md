# Personal Budget Analysis

**Tools:** Excel, Tableau, Python  
**Dataset:** 6 months of personal transactions

## Question
Which spending categories consistently exceed budget, and where is there room to save?

## Key Findings
- Food is the largest expense at 4438 PLN (29% of total spending)
- 4 categories exceeded budget: Food, Dining Out, Transport, Shopping
- Overall spending was 83 PLN under budget due to overestimation in Utilities and Health & Fitness
